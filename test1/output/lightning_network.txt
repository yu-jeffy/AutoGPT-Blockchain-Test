Notes on the Lightning Network: \nOverview:  The lightning network is a Layer 2 solution allowing for rapid and cheap transactions with users bypassing blockchain verification for extra speed.\\n Advantages and Disadvantages: The Lightning Network provides two main advantages like speed and cheap transaction fees. However, there are some disadvantages such as centralization along with the risk of double-spending attempts.\\n Implementation and adoption progress: It is currently in use in the Bitcoin network and other blockchain networks like Litecoin, Bitcoin Cash, among others, with over 25,000 Lightning Network nodes and around 130,000 payment channels. Major players like Twitter and Square have also invested in the technology. \\n Lightning Network clients: LND (Lightning Network Daemon), c-lightning and Eclair.
