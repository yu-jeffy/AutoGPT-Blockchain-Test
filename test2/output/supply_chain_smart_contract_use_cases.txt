As a supplier, I can register my products with the contract.
As a supplier, I can verify that my products have been received by the distributor.
As a distributor, I can verify that the products received from the supplier match what was registered.
As a distributor, I can register the products received from the supplier.
As a retailer, I can verify that the products received from the distributor match what was registered.
As a retailer, I can register the products received from the distributor.
As a consumer, I can verify the authenticity of a product by scanning its barcode.
As an auditor, I can verify the authenticity and history of a product by querying the blockchain.
